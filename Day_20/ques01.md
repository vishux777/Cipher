### Question-01

You are given an image in n\*m matrix now you have to rotate the image by 90 degree.

**Sample Input:**

```
Enter the number of rows and columns: 3 3
Enter the elements of the image matrix:
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9
```

**Sample Output:**

```
Rotated image by 90 degrees:
7 4 1
8 5 2
9 6 3
```
